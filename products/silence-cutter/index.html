Questions?

Why is the app not opening?

Because Silence Cutter is distributed outside the Mac App Store, macOS may show a security warning on first launch. Simply right-click the app and select "Open" to bypass it. This only needs to be done once.

What video formats are supported?

Silence Cutter supports all formats that macOS can play natively — including MP4, MOV, M4V, and most common codecs like H.264 and H.265. If QuickTime can open it, Silence Cutter can process it.

What macOS version do I need?

Silence Cutter requires macOS 15 (Sequoia) or later. It runs natively on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.

Is this a subscription?

Nope! Buy it once, use it forever. No recurring charges, no hidden fees.

Can I use it with Final Cut Pro or Premiere?

Yes! Silence Cutter can export FCPXML for Final Cut Pro, EDL for DaVinci Resolve, and Premiere Pro XML — so you can do the silence cutting in Silence Cutter and finish the edit in your preferred NLE.

Does it work without internet?

Absolutely. Silence Cutter is 100% offline. Your videos never leave your Mac — no uploads, no cloud processing, no data collected.
